Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the Minecraft server?

Simply add PLAY.HORIZION.IN to your Minecraft server list. We support Minecraft versions 1.8 to 1.21.

How can I purchase ranks?

Ranks can be purchased through our Discord server. Join our Discord and check the #store channel for more information.

What game modes are available?

We offer Survival, Bedwars, and various Arcade mini-games. Check out our homepage for more details about each game mode.

How do I report a player?

You can report players in-game using the /report command, or join our Discord server and use the #report channel.
